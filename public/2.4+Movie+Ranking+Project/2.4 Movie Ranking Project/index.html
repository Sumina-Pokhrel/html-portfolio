Top 3 movies of all-time.

suzume

“Suzume” (2022) is a Japanese animated fantasy adventure film directed by Makoto Shinkai. It follows 17-year-old Suzume Iwato and a mysterious young man named Souta Munakata as they journey across Japan to close supernatural doors releasing disasters. The film blends action, adventure, and fantasy with stunning animation and a compelling emotional narrative. Suzume’s journey is about confronting her past and finding inner strength.

A Walk to Remember

It is a heartfelt romance film where a rebellious teenager, Landon Carter, falls in love with Jamie Sullivan, a quiet girl with leukemia. As their relationship deepens, Landon learns valuable life lessons and transforms into a better person. Jamie’s illness brings a poignant twist to their love story, making it both inspiring and tragic. This movie beautifully captures themes of love, faith, and redemption. 💖

Inside Out

is an animated film that explores the emotions inside the mind of an 11-year-old girl named Riley. As she navigates a move to a new city, her emotions—Joy, Sadness, Fear, Disgust, and Anger—struggle to help her cope with the changes. The movie beautifully illustrates how all emotions are essential for a balanced life. It’s a touching and imaginative journey that resonates with both children and adults.
